Best Banana Chocolate Chip Muffins

Original Recipe

Tricks to Make the Recipe Better

  1. Use a silicone muffin pan to ensure even cooking.
  2. Use a quarter cup more chocolate chips than the recipe calls for.
  3. Grease your muffin pans with butter to keep the muffins moist.
  4. Mix ingredients by hand to avoid overmixing.
  5. The riper the bananas, the more flavorful the muffins.

Paraphrased Baking Instructions

  • Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and warm the silicone muffin trays for easier greasing.
  • Mix the ingredients in a large bowl, fully mashing the bananas before adding other ingredients.
  • Add chocolate chips last and avoid overmixing to maintain color and texture.

Ingredients and Quantities

  • 4 ripe bananas (1 & 1/3 cups mashed)
  • 1/3 cup melted butter, cooled
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose or gluten-free flour
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Order of Ingredient Pairing

  1. Mashed bananas and melted butter
  2. Sugar, egg, and vanilla
  3. Baking soda and salt
  4. Flour
  5. Chocolate chips
